Have you forgotten someone today?

We know that life is busy and sometimes, before you know it, the day has slipped away! Use our handy calendar function to remind you when it's time to check in on a loved one.

  • (1) - Select the date when you want to set a reminder.
  • (2) - Enter your reminder message, e.g. "Tell Mum I'm thinking of her".
  • (3) - The date will turn red on the days when you have a reminder set.
